AIFS has been approved to deliver Food Handler and Food Safety Supervisor training in the Australian state of Victoria.

If you are looking to complete a food safety training course that will be recognised in Melbourne, and throughout the entire state of Victoria, you can register for the official [AIFS Food Handler Course](/courses/food-handler-course) or the [AIFS Food Safety Supervisor Course](/courses/food-safety-supervisor). The courses are delivered completely online and can be completed from the comfort of your own home.
